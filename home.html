About Me

I'm an avid reader, my favourite books being Wizard's First Rule by Terry Goodkind, the Skulduggery Pleasant series by Derek Landy, and the Heroes of Olympus series by Rick Riordan. I am also a self-proclaimed metalhead, and have started dabbling in making my own music. I spend much of my time behind a screen, and if I'm not there, then I'll be in the kitchen or working out.

My interest in coding comes from my love of video games and all things related to the internet. I spend quite a large portion of my waking hours on the interenet,and the creativity and skills that goes into making the content that I consume fascinates me.

I would describe myself as respectful, open and accomodating. I am always ready to take on a challenge and to help anyone to the best of my ability. I work hard and can perform under pressure. I have a storied history of meeting deadlines: if I can create something up to standard within the deadline, then I will do it, but I will not hesitate to negotiate an extension if I do not believe that I can produce quality work before the deadline.

I am introspective and observant by nature, and this sometimes comes across as antisocial behaviour. I don't initially feel comfortable in large groups, but will form strong bonds given time. I believe that I would be a great addition to any team because of my calm demeanour and leadership qualities, as well as creative problem solving skills that I have honed during my studies and the current course at Life Choices Academy.
